Slumberland

"Slumberland" is a comic book series written by Shaun Simon and illustrated by Michael Allred. The story follows a narcoleptic DJ named Johnny who embarks on a surreal journey to find the ultimate dream: the "Radium Halogen Dream Lamp." Along the way, he encounters various eccentric characters and navigates through bizarre dreamscapes. The series combines elements of fantasy, adventure, and humor, with vibrant artwork and imaginative storytelling. "Slumberland" has garnered praise for its creative premise and unique visual style, offering readers an immersive and entertaining experience.
